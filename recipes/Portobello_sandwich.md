# Portobello sandwich

`summer` `roasted red pepper` `mushrooms` `sandwich` `red bell pepper` `Vegetarian` `Portobello` `Mozzarella`

Remove gills from mushrooms, clean, slice as thinly as possible. Heat olive oil in cast iron pan over medium heat, add sliced mushrooms \(in batches if needed, do not crowd\), salt lightly , cook relatively undisturbed until golden brown. Add 1 clove minced garlic and a generous pour of balsamic vinegar and cook down until vinegar thick and saucey.Â 

Mix mushrooms with roasted red peppers and ripped basil. Add salt and pepper. Place on toasted bread with fresh mozzarella. Yum
