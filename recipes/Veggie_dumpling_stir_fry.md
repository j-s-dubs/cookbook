# Veggie dumpling stir fry

`broccoli` `Vegetarian` `Carrots` `Sriracha` `Miso` `Dumplings` `Vegan`

Carrots, chopped into 1/2 in chunks 

Broccoli, chopped into small stalks 

Green onions, finely sliced 

Sliced cabbage 

1 bag vegetable dumplings

1 recipe miso ginger dressing 

Sriracha 

In the style of Green Symphony dumplings

Steam together carrots and broccoli, sprinkled with salt. Sauté/ steam dumplings according to directions on bag. Add cabbage and cover until slightly wilted. Add carrots, broccoli, green onions. Serve with miso dressing and sriracha.
